About the Couple

Camille Schultz

Bride

Lewiston, MN

One day, Camille decided she was going to move to Austin on a whim. The art and music festivals combined with the general lack of snow and ice made the move an easy decision. A few days after moving there, her friend, Cheryl, persuaded her to go to karaoke night at the local 290 West Club in Austin. She was convinced Camille would like the bartender, Sean. She was right...

The two had a lot to talk about since they both recently moved to Austin for many of the same reasons. Later they found they shared the same birthday (they still can't decide if this is a good thing or not).

That summer, Sean introduced her to paintball, the Austin City Limits festival, tubing down the San Marcos river, the Hamilton Pool, and anything that made it seem she was on a permanent vacation. She loved to bring homemade enchiladas to him at the bar, and in return, her drinks were always on the house.

They love taking on way too many hobbies than they have time for, going to movies at the Alamo Drafthouse, and trying to build the furniture they want. The couple live in South Austin with their infamous labrador, Vera.

Sean O'Toole-Pitts

Groom

El Paso, TX

Why May 5th?

Sean chose Thursday, May 5th for the wedding to honor the birthday of his grandfather, James O'Toole. The story of how he met and fell in love with his wife, Maria Dolores, in Mexico is amazing considering neither knew the other's language. They were married in Chihuahua, Mexico in November 1955.
